Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Facts

  • Cook Time: 30 minutes
  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour
  • Servings: 8
  • Yield: 1 9x13 inch pan

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz) Package of Lasagna
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 (16 oz) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 glove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 lb shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 lb shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Once boiling, add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In large skillet over medium-high, brown beef and seaon with salt and pepper; drain.
  4. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic; simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, cheddar and ricotta cheese; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of pasta, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filed.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Enjoy!
